An **object** is a collection of **key-value pairs**.

```js

  name: "John",
  age: 30,
  isStudent: false

### ðŸ”¸ Accessing Values:

### ðŸ”¸ Modifying Values:

```

### ðŸ”¸ Looping through an Object:
